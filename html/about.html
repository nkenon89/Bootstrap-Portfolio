My Story

From FIT Pro to I.T Pro

Hello!
My name is Nick, I am a 29 year old former health and fitness coach turned I.T professional and web developer. After several years of waning interest in my field I decided in May of 2018 that I was going tochallenge myself to make a change!

I had very much enjoyed my nearly ten year long career in health and fitness- but Ineeded something more!

I craved something more intellectually stimulating. Something where I could be creativeand challenged by my work. Something that could offer me a more sustainable career path and financialstability.

After a couple months of research and some web development self study I decided to invest in myselfand my new career and attend the UNC Coding Bootcamp. It was a 6 month program focused on full stack javascriptdeveloping. Throughout the course of the program we were exposed to everything from basic html markup to advancedjavascript libraries such as React.

Over the course of the past year I have learned an incredible amount about afield I initially knew nothing about! This process has been exhilarating and left me wanting even more. Web developmenthas been a steep learning curve and I imagine it will continue to be, but this type of challenge is exactly what I waslooking for!

I am still green, but I am passionate and excited to learn more. I am teachable and absorb newinformation quickly.
